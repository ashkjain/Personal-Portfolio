About Me

Hello! I'm Ashish Jain, a passionate Web Backend Developer originally from New Delhi, India. Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Brigham Young University - Idaho in Rexburg, Idaho.
My main focus is on backend development, where I continuously learn and refine my skills. I have a good grasp of several programming languages and technologies, including JavaScript, Python, MySQL, C#, CSS, HTML, and Flask. I'm also a certified Python Entry Level Developer (PCEP-30-02).
I enjoy the challenges of backend development, from database management to server-side logic. I'm always eager to explore new technologies and enhance my skillset. Besides coding, I love sharing my knowledge and experiences through my blog, helping others navigate the ever-evolving world of technology.
